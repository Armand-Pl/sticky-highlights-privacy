Privacy Policy for Sticky Highlights

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Sticky Highlights ("the extension", "we", "us") is a Chrome browser extension that lets you highlight, annotate, and save passages of text on webpages. This policy explains what data the extension handles, how it is stored, and what control you have over it.

We have written this policy to describe exactly what the extension does today. If that changes — for example if we add an optional cloud sync feature — we will update this policy first and tell you about it inside the extension. See the "Future changes" section below for details.

What data the extension collects

When you select text on a webpage and choose to highlight it, the extension stores:

  • The highlighted text itself
  • Any note or tags you choose to add to that highlight
  • The color you assigned to the highlight
  • The URL and title of the page the highlight was made on
  • The date and time the highlight was created

The extension also stores your own configuration choices, so they persist between browser sessions:

  • The list of websites you have enabled or disabled (your whitelist or blacklist)
  • Your default highlight color
  • Your custom Markdown export templates, if you have changed them from the default

We do not collect any data beyond what is listed above. We do not collect your browsing history, your identity, analytics about your usage, or any data from websites you have not explicitly chosen to enable the extension on.

How your data is stored

As of this version, all data stays on your device. The extension uses Chrome's built-in local storage (chrome.storage.local) only. There is no remote server, no account, and no login. Nothing is transmitted over the internet by the extension.

This means:

  • Your highlights are only accessible from the Chrome profile where you created them
  • If you use Chrome on another device, your highlights will not appear there unless Chrome's own bookmark/profile sync is enabled at the operating system level (which is a Google Chrome feature, not something this extension controls)
  • Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all locally stored data

Data we never collect

We do not, under any circumstance:

  • Sell your data to any third party
  • Share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Use your data for personalized advertising
  • Track your browsing activity on sites where the extension is not enabled
  • Run any analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting service
  • Load any third-party script or tracking pixel inside the extension

Why the extension requests the permissions it does

Exporting your highlights

The extension lets you export your highlights as Markdown, JSON, or CSV files. These files are generated and downloaded directly to your own device by your browser. We never see, receive, or have access to any exported file — the export happens entirely locally.

Your control over your data

  • You can delete any individual highlight at any time from the side panel
  • You can delete all of your highlights at once from Settings → Danger zone → Delete all highlights
  • You can remove a site from your whitelist (or add it to your blacklist) at any time, which immediately stops the extension from running on that site
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all of its stored data from your browser

Children's privacy

Sticky Highlights is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Future changes — cloud sync

We are considering adding an optional cloud sync feature in a future version, so that highlights can be backed up and accessed across devices. If and when this is introduced:

  • It will be clearly presented as an optional, opt-in feature — never enabled automatically
  • This privacy policy will be updated before the feature is released, to disclose exactly what data would be sent, to which service, and how it is secured
  • You will be able to use the extension exactly as it works today, fully locally, without ever activating sync

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any question about this policy or how the extension handles your data, contact us at stickyhighlights@outlook.com.
